privacy policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Seedless Labs, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Seedless," "we," "us," or "our"), handles information in connection with the Seedless wallet application, the website at seedlesslabs.xyz, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Who we are

Seedless Labs, Inc. is a Delaware C corporation that builds a non-custodial passkey wallet for the Solana network. We do not hold, control, or have access to your funds, your private keys, or your passkey credentials. Your passkey is generated and stored on your device using your operating system's secure enclave or equivalent hardware-backed keystore.

2. Information we do not collect

We have designed the Service so that we collect as little personal information as possible. We do not collect or store:

  • Seed phrases, mnemonics, or private keys (none are ever generated)
  • Passkey credentials or the biometric data used to authorize them
  • Personal identifiers such as full name, government ID, or address (unless you voluntarily submit them, e.g., for a beta waitlist or grant program)

3. Information we may collect

In limited cases, we may receive the following:

  • Public wallet addresses. When you use the Service to send, receive, or sign transactions, the resulting on-chain activity is recorded on the Solana blockchain, which is public by design.
  • Beta waitlist submissions. If you join the private mainnet beta, we collect the contact details you provide (e.g., email, X handle, wallet address) through our intake form so we can send you access instructions and updates.
  • Diagnostic information. Anonymous, aggregated logs from RPC providers and crash reporting may be retained to debug and improve reliability. These are not used to identify individual users.

4. Third-party services

The Service relies on third-party infrastructure to function. Your use of the Service may involve interactions with:

  • Solana RPC providers (e.g., Helius, Alchemy, public mainnet endpoints)
  • Passkey infrastructure provided by LazorKit
  • Optional privacy infrastructure provided by Umbra
  • Gasless transaction relayers (e.g., Kora)
  • Hosting and analytics providers (e.g., Vercel) for the website

Each of these third parties operates under its own privacy policy. We do not control how they handle data they receive directly from your device or browser.

5. How we use information

Any information we do receive is used solely to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Communicate with users who have voluntarily contacted us or joined the beta waitlist
  • Diagnose technical issues and prevent abuse
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for advertising.

6. Blockchain data

Transactions on Solana are public, permanent, and outside our control. Once a transaction is broadcast, the associated public address and transaction details are visible to anyone with access to the network. We cannot delete, modify, or restrict access to on-chain data.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

8. Security

Because Seedless is non-custodial, the security of your funds depends primarily on the security of the device on which you generated your passkey. We strongly recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date and protecting it with a screen lock, biometric lock, or equivalent.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will also be communicated through the Service or through our social channels.
