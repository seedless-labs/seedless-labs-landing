How It Works

Three steps. No seed phrases. No gas fees.

1. Authenticate

Create your wallet with FaceID, TouchID, or fingerprint. Your biometrics are your keys. Nothing to write down, nothing to lose.

2. Transact

Send SOL, swap tokens via Jupiter, or receive payments privately. All transactions are sponsored - zero gas fees.

3. Stay Private

Use stealth addresses for anonymous receiving. Spin up burner wallets with zero on-chain link. Privacy is the default.

$SEED Manifesto

The Problem

Seed phrases were a mistake we all accepted.

12 words written on paper. Hidden in a drawer. Lost in a house fire. Forgotten after three months. This was never going to scale. We just got used to it because there was no alternative.

And gas fees? Asking users to hold SOL just to send SOL? That's not onboarding. That's a barrier.

Seedless Wallet exists because crypto UX is still broken — and we're fixing it.

What We're Building

Seedless is a Solana wallet built for normal people:

  • Passkey authentication. Your face or fingerprint. No 12 words.
  • Gasless transactions. Send, swap, and sign without holding SOL for fees.
  • Privacy built-in. Stealth addresses and burner wallets. Defaults, not add-ons.
  • Jupiter swaps. Best prices across all DEXes. Zero gas.

This is what a wallet should've been from day one.

Why $SEED Matters

$SEED is how we align incentives with the community:

  • Launched on Bags.fm — community-driven from the start. No insider allocations.
  • Revenue share — a portion of swap fees goes back to holders.
  • Early access — holders get new features first.

We don't control the supply. We don't control the price. Conviction does.

$SEED isn't speculation. It's participation.

How Funds Will Be Used

Everything raised goes into shipping:

  • iOS build — currently Android only, iOS is next
  • Solana Seeker support — mobile-native Solana hardware
  • Transaction history — full on-chain activity view
  • Multi-token support — beyond SOL and USDC
  • Better recovery flows — because passkeys need backup options too

The goal is simple: make Seedless the default wallet for anyone who thinks seed phrases are stupid.
