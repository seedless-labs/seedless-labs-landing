about

why seedless labs

prediction markets work better when positions are private. wallets work better when there's nothing to lose. privacy isn't a feature you add later - it's the foundation you build on.

seedless removes the biggest friction in crypto - seed phrases and gas fees. login with your fingerprint. send money without paying fees. receive payments to addresses no one can trace back to you.

parasite takes that privacy layer and applies it to prediction markets. bet on anything happening on solana or in the real world. no one sees your position. no whale tracking. no copy trading. just conviction.

the protocol feeds off existing infrastructure - pyth, switchboard, kalshi, jupiter - and grows with the ecosystem. the more solana builds, the more markets parasite can offer.

built solo. shipped daily. brick by brick.

infrastructure

what powers this

building on battle-tested infrastructure. no custom programs. no audit delays. just composing what already works.

authentication

lazorkit passkey sdk. biometric login. no seed phrases. device secure enclave.

gasless

kora paymaster sponsors transaction fees. users never pay gas.

price feeds

pyth network for real-time crypto prices. oracle-verified settlement.

custom data

switchboard for on-chain metrics. permissionless oracle creation.

real-world events

kalshi api for regulated prediction markets. elections, finance, sports.

swaps

jupiter aggregator for token swaps. best routes across solana dexes.
